About Me

Second Year Undergraduate Student

I am currently a student at Washington University in St. Louis pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science + Mathematics. To me, technology serves as an integral part of our modern world as it plays a very important role in connecting vast communities with one another. From social media platforms to messaging applications and even simple phone calls, technology is what keeps the billions of people in our society interconnected. This was especially evident at the start of COVID-19, and this interconnectivity is what inspired me to pursue computer science.


Outside of academics, my interests lie in playing video games, finding new music to listen to, hitting up the badminton courts with my friends, traveling, and trying new foods (follow @twojiaozi on Instagram)!
